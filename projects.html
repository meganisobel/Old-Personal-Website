Art Everyday iOS Mobile App

June 2020

For the final project of Harvard CS50's Introduction to Computer Science course I decided to make an iOS app to encourage people to paint or draw everyday.

The app provides a random image from a bank of nature themed images and a colour palette extracted from the chosen image. This is designed to help artists to mix colours representative of the image.

There are two modes: 'Michelangelo' and 'Cézanne'. Michelangelo mode provides an unfiltered image with true colours. Cézanne mode provides a blue toned images and palettes. There is an optional grid-lines feature to divide the image into sections.
